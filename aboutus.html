About us

We are a group of Year 8 students from Morado, driven by our passion for art and culture. Together, we've established the Myanmar Art Gallery to showcase the beauty of our country's heritage. Through carefully curated exhibits featuring traditional crafts, famous landmarks, and diverse artworks, we aim to inspire appreciation and understanding of Myanmar's rich cultural legacy. Join us as we celebrate our artistic heritage and share its wonders with the world.
