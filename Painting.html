Recycled-1708

The “Recycled” series is a very personal project for Htein Lin. When he was in prison, he created many works on A4 paper in secret, which he managed to covertly get out and sent to his wife to get them to safety. Unfortunately, these works ended up at the paper recycler. Upon his release from prison, Htein Lin bought the cardboards from recycling, thinking there was some of his work inside. Since then, when an emotion touches him, an event disturbs him - or rejoices him, he draws and restores his thoughts on these "recycled". We have here a tender evocation of Lotus' flowers on the Irrawaddy River.

Young Horseman

In the midst of a vast, open field, a figure emerges with a regal posture atop a spirited brown steed. The rider, adorned in traditional attire of vibrant reds and greens with golden embellishments, becomes one with the horse as they move in unison. The horse's muscles ripple beneath its glossy coat, a testament to the creature's strength and the speed of their gallop. The soft hues of the background trees blur into a gentle haze, creating a sense of depth and movement. This painting, signed by the artist, captures not just a moment but the essence of a timeless cultural narrative, evoking a sense of history and tradition that transcends the canvas.

May Myo Flowers

In this captivating artwork, a bouquet of flowers bursts forth with life and color. The petals, in hues of delicate pink, pure white, and passionate red, are painted with such care that they seem to dance in an unseen breeze. Each flower is rendered with a unique touch, their blossoms ranging from tightly furled buds to fully unfurled blooms that invite the viewer to ponder their intricate forms. The dark backdrop serves as a dramatic contrast, highlighting the vivid colors and allowing the subtle gradations of light and shadow to play across the leaves and petals. The artist's brush strokes are both precise and spirited, adding a sense of movement and vitality to the still life. This painting is not just a visual treat; it's a celebration of nature's transient beauty, masterfully captured on canvas.

Anada Temple

The Ananda Temple, located in the heart of the ancient city of Bagan, Myanmar, is a testament to the architectural grandeur of the Pagan Dynasty. This image captures the temple's golden spires reaching towards the sky, a hallmark of its design that has inspired awe for centuries. The temple's facade is adorned with intricate carvings and statues that reflect the rich spiritual heritage of Myanmar. Surrounded by verdant foliage, the Ananda Temple stands as a serene oasis, inviting a diverse throng of visitors who come to bask in its sacred ambiance. Above, the sky is painted with gentle strokes of blue and white, suggesting a peaceful day perfect for reflection and reverence at this historic site. The Ananda Temple is not just a marvel of religious significance; it's a cultural beacon that continues to draw the admiration of pilgrims and travelers alike.

Bagan

Sagaing

This vibrant artwork captures the essence of a city in Myanmar, where tradition and nature intertwine gracefully. Carved staircases wind through the rugged landscape, leading to temples that rise majestically with golden spires piercing the sky. The sun casts a gentle glow, illuminating the intricate designs of the architecture and the serene expressions of red-robed monks in quiet contemplation. Lush greenery frames the scene, a testament to the city's harmony with its natural surroundings. The rich tapestry of colors, from the earthy tones of the stone to the radiant gold of the temples and the monks' vivid attire, creates a sense of peace and spiritual richness that is quintessentially Myanmar.

The Cloud And The Sea

The image presents a whimsical and abstract interpretation of a seascape, where the sky and sea converge in a dance of colors and shapes. Dominating the upper portion, stylized clouds outlined in a gentle red hue float serenely against a backdrop of soft turquoise, reminiscent of a calm sky on a warm day. These clouds, with their repetitive patterns, evoke a sense of rhythm and movement, as if they are adrift on a gentle breeze. Below, the sea is depicted in a contrasting greyish-blue tone, its stillness suggesting the quiet depths of the ocean. Separating these two realms is a thin strip of green, peppered with smaller, multi-colored cloud-like forms, serving as an abstract horizon line that anchors the composition. The image, devoid of any distinct landmarks or figures, invites the viewer to interpret the scene through the lens of their imagination, where the boundaries between the sky and sea blur into a harmonious blend of nature’s elements.xx`

The image captures the essence of a tranquil seascape, where the boundary between the sea and sky is a dance of colors and forms. Inya Lake

Inya Lake, a jewel of Myanmar, is beautifully depicted in this artistic rendering. The image is a tranquil composition of sailboats adrift on the placid waters, their white sails a stark contrast to the myriad blues that dance across the canvas. The lighter shades of blue merge into the sky, while the deeper tones suggest the lake's depths. Visible brushstrokes add texture and movement, reminiscent of the gentle breezes that sweep across Inya Lake. This piece, signed by the artist 'Kumar' in '91, captures the essence of a serene day out on the water, where time seems to stand still and nature's beauty is in full display. The artwork is a testament to the peaceful and calming atmosphere that Inya Lake offers to both locals and visitors alike.

The Alms Walk

In the tranquil embrace of nature, a trio of monks robed in the vivid hues of devotion traverse a meandering path. Their journey, a silent sermon of humility, is marked by the alms bowls they carry, symbols of their reliance on the generosity of others. The verdant greenery that flanks their walk whispers tales of life's perpetual renewal, while the open skies above promise boundless possibilities. A white edifice, a silent sentinel, looms in the distance, its presence a testament to the spiritual sanctuary that anchors their existence. This painting, a harmonious symphony of color and spirit, captures the essence of a sacred tradition that transcends the mere act of alms gathering, inviting the observer into a moment of reflection on the interplay between humanity and the divine.

Ascension Amidst Autumn

In the heart of a tranquil forest, where the whispers of autumn rustle through the fallen leaves, a solitary monk embarks on a silent pilgrimage. Clad in the deep saffron robes of his monastic order, he ascends an ancient stone staircase, each step a testament to his unwavering devotion. The trees, bare as if in reverence, bow their intricate branches overhead, forming a natural archway that leads towards enlightenment. This sacred journey, set against the backdrop of nature’s serene beauty, is a powerful portrayal of the monk's quest for spiritual elevation amidst the ephemeral world.
