Crafts

Handicrafts in Myanmar are undervalued. Made with much precision and care, Myanmar craftwork is a form of art that represents the authentic expertise and skill of its people. From stone-carving, lacquerware, umbrella-making to marionettes, each craft has a long history of glory, with some dating back to the early 1800s. As one of Myanmar’s hidden treasures, these crafts make up the country’s cultural identity as well as heritag.
